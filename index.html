Originally formed in 2011, Relay Tapes is the musical project of Jade Tyers. Following the release of the Semblance EP on Berlin-based label Dreams Never End in 2018, Relay Tapes expanded into the live performance realm with the addition of players from Brisbane bands including Ultra Material and PYNES. In 2020, Shore Dive Records (UK) released Early Morning Abstract, the follow-up EP to Semblance.
